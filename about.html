WHO WE ARE

Next Gen STEM is a completely free resource for students to learn the basics of coding! Our organization was built off of the fact that students across the country and world do not have the proper resources to learn how to code despite its overwhelming prevalence across job fields today.

In a study performed by Google, 9 out of 10 parents believed that Computer Science education would be a good use of resources at schools and over 60% of superintendents and principals agree that computer science courses should be valued the same as common courses including math, science, history, and English. Yet, about 40% of 9,774 principles surveyed said that their schools offer 0 computer science courses at their school.

Next Gen STEM is constantly working to provide students with more and more resources and ways to learn how to code. Whether through courses that are taught through live instruction, video lessons, written lessons, or a mix of all of the above, students will be provided with the STEM education that works best for them at no cost.
